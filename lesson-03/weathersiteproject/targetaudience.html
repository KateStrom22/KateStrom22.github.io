Target Audience

Description of target audience: Target audience would include anyone who wants to know about Preston, specifically relating to weather and attractions.

Two examples of people who would use the site and why, what they'd expect, etc: One example of someone who would the site could be an uncle planning a family BBQ on Saturday, he would expect a weather forecast to let him know how Saturday was looking weather wise. Another example of someone who could use the site could be a Dad planning to take his family through Preston on a road trip in a few weeks, checking local places to visit, the map, and weather.

At least 5 listed scenarios:
1: Someone on their way to work needs the weather outside to know if they need an umbrella to get to work.
2: Someone is planning a visit to a water park in a week and wants to know what day is the nicest to go.
3: Someone will be traveling through Preston and wants to know the weater and good spots to visit.
4: Someone planning to attend a baseball game and wondering if they will need a sweater next week.
5: Someone who plans to go golfing and wants to know if it will rain.
