Typography

Explain my typography choices and show what I picked for what:
I have been told to pick typography. I have not, however, been told what it is being applied to. I have no idea how many things I will have, so I will do my best to guess and adjust as I go. Same applies to font size, this is infinitely easier to pick once actually put on a page and you can judge visually in comparison to everything else. I picked Ar Julian because it is a bigger chunkier font that grabs the attention easily, and Century Gothic because it is a clean crisp professional modern font.

Main header font: Ar Julian, 24
Moto font: Century Gothic, 14
Nav menu font: Century Gothic Bold, 12
Heading(body): Ar Julian, 16
Main text(body): Century Gothic, 12
Footer: Century Gothic, 10
